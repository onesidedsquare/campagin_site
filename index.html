A Bit About Steven

I’m a proud Woodstock resident and have spent the last few years building a life here with my wife, Kimberly (of 20 years), and our three kids (17, 13, and 10; all proud CCSD students!). We’ve also got a full house with our lab, Bucky Barnes, and our geckos, Bane and Chip!

Since moving here, I’ve jumped headfirst into our community. You’ve probably seen me volunteering with the PTA (Booth/Etowah), working with the Scouts, or even at the table for a game of D&D/Pathfinder. Whether we’re hiking our local trails or seeing you at church, it’s been an honor to be part of this community.

Now, I want to give back. I’m running for the Board of Commissioners to ensure your voice is heard, not just the out-of-state developers who are clearing our trees and packing our roads. It’s time to focus on the real work that matters to us.

I’m excited for the opportunity to represent you at the county level!
